Stephanie Chinn's Profile

This page will be a profile page describing Stephanie Chinn as a candidate for an Electronic Resources Librarian position. The site will be largely text-based, and will include a professional-style photograph of Stephanie. Some sample content is included below.

Stephanie has been working in libraries for over 7 years with experience in both public and academic libraries. She earned her Master of Library & Information Science in 2019 from the University of Missouri.

This degree is part of the library and information science program, housed in the School of Information Science & Learning Technologies (SISLT). The program is accredited by the American Library Association (ALA), which granted continued accreditation status in 2015.

Prior to enrolling in graduate school, Stephanie worked as a paraprofessional at the Rolla Public Library and then at the Curtis Laws Wilson Library.

In addition to her studies in graduate school, Stephanie remains dedictated to ongoing professional development through webinars, training sessions, and attending conferences such as:

  • MLA (Missouri Library Association)
  • MOBIUS
  • ER&L (Electronic Resources & Libraries)

Stephanie is also a member of professional organizations:

  • American Library Association
  • Missouri Library Association

For more information, you may contact Stephanie at garvin@mail.missouri.edu.
