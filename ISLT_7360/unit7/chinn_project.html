With over 7 years of library experience, Stephanie Chinn is able to envision librarianship from many different facets. Throughout her career in both public and academic libraries, she has maintained her passion for connecting patrons with the right information to meet their needs. She has served in pubic facing roles as well as focusing on the organization of information in order to always put patrons and their quests for information first.

A strong familiarity with MARC records and OCLC allow Stephanie to troubleshoot accessibility issues and streamline processes and procedures for maximum productivity. Her extensive background in project management and finance, coupled with skills in statistical analysis are beneficial in ascertaining that library resources are being utilized in the most cost-effective manner.

Ongoing research interests include:

  • Information accessibility
  • User experience
  • Emerging technologies in library systems
